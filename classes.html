THE CLASSES WE OFFER!

Cardio Class

(Monday and Wednesday)

Boost endurance and burn calories with our high-energy Cardio Class. This session features interval training, running drills, and dynamic body movements to keep your heart pumping strong.

Strength Class

(Tuesday and Thursday)

Build power and muscle definition in our Strength Class. This session focuses on resistance training, weightlifting techniques, and controlled movements to enhance strength and conditioning.

Hybrid Class

(Friday, Saturday and Sunday)

Experience the best of both worlds with our Hybrid Class! Combining cardio and strength elements, this session features an electrifying workout with a live DJ to keep you motivated and energized.

* During the class if you accommodation due to injury or disability, speak with a coach on site and accommodations will be made.
